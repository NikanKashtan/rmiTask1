Located in the upper reaches of the river Isar in Munich (Munchen, Minga, Monacum, Monachium)
is the capital of Bavaria,the third largest city in the country and the second
most visited tourist attraction (after Berlin).The characteristic "fabulous" architecture,
an abundance of monuments and museums, cheerful folk and traditional
Bavarian hospitality (Gemutlichkeit), a rich industrial history and beautiful surroundings,
abundance of pubs and restaurants, luxury shopping and brilliant nightlife
- All this is just "playing into the hands" of this beautiful city.
